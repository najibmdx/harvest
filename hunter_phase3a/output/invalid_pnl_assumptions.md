# Invalid PnL Assumptions

- A transfer is not automatically a buy or sell.
- A deposit is not automatically a buy.
- A withdrawal is not automatically a sell.
- historicalUSD is not automatically cost basis.
- current balance value is not realized PnL.
- balance delta is not realized PnL unless acquisition/disposal evidence is proven.
- CEX interaction is not automatically a trade.
- entity label does not prove trade intent.
- unknown transfer direction cannot support realized PnL.
- token exposure does not prove profitability.
